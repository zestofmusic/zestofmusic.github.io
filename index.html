About Me

With over 9+ years in the music industry, I am a passionate music producer, audio engineer, and piano teacher. My journey in music has been driven by a deep love for sound and a commitment to helping artists and students achieve their musical aspirations.

I completed a Pro Music Production course at Mumbai Music Institute (MMI) in 2019, which significantly honed my technical skills and creative approach. A particularly memorable milestone was performing live with legendary music composer Pritam Da as part of a Guinness World Record 1,000-piece band – an incredible honor that broadened my perspective on large-scale musical productions.

My professional experience includes working as a music assistant with Abhijeet Sawant (first Indian Idol winner), serving as an audio engineer for Sony TV, producing music for labels like Saregama India and Zee Music artists, and collaborating with a diverse range of label artists and independent musicians.

My goal is to help you bring your sound to life.
